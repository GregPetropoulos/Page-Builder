My Favorite Activity

One of my favorite activities is to go tour art mueseums. I enjoy discovering new artist, and interpreting the different mediums artist use to express themselves. Art mueseums are also a great reflection to what socioeconomic and cultural topics are prevalent during the present. They cherish the past, honor the present, and can foreshadow the future of our society.
